Popular Cricket Bets

Beginners to cricket betting can quickly become overwhelmed. This brief overview of popular cricket bets will give you a preview of the options you have when you start betting on this exciting sport.

1
Match Winner
This is the simplest bet for cricket betting. This wager involves you betting on the team that you think will emerge as the winner after the match. Since there are only two teams involved in the match, you can decide on either Team A or Team B.
2
Series Winner
This bet involves more than just one match. Instead, you will be waiting for the entire series to be over before you will know if you win. In this bet, you will be betting on the team that will be victorious at the end of the entire series.
3
Outright Winner
In this bet, you will be wagering on the team that wins the overall tournament. It goes beyond just one match or one series. Since there are many options to bet on, it is a risky bet but with a higher payout.
4
Tied Match
As the name suggests, placing a tied match bet means you predict that the match will either end in a tie or not. It is not as uncommon as you think! With two options: yes or no, the chance of winning is 50%.
5
Draw No Bet Wager
Placing a wager on this bet means you think there is a good chance the match will draw but want to bet on a winning team. In this bet, you pick a winning team but if it ends in a draw, you get your bet back. Essentially, you have insurance if thee match ends in a tie.
6
Top Batsman
In this bet, you pick a player you think will score the most runs. This bet can be based on a set timeframe. This means you can bet on the top batsman for an inning, match, or even entire series.
7
Top Bowler
You will be betting on the player that you think will take the most wickets. Like the top batsman bet, the top bowler bet is also based on a set timeframe. You can bet on the top bowler just for one inning, one match, or entire series.
8
Man of the Match
At the end of a cricket match, there is one player who will receive this title. This title is given to the most outstanding player or the player who makes the most impact in the entire match. This bet involves you wagering on the player you think will win this honor.
9
To Win The Toss
In cricket, a coin toss determines which captain of the team wins the right to choose whether their team will field or bat at the start of the match. In this bet, you only have two options, either Team A or Team B.
10
Live Betting / In-Play Bets
In-play betting is increasingly popular as punters get to evaluate the game before placing a bet. In this type of bet, you place wagers as the match is going on. This type of bet is very exciting as it can be a great way to feel more involved in the action.
