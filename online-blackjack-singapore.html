This is the most popular betting strategy for a wide variety of different casino games. Players double their stake every time they lose. Once you win, you reset your bets to base. This method is based on the probability that the more times you lose, you are bound to win the next round.

The original variant follows the basic rules with both player and banker battling to get the best hand without exceeding 21 points.

Usually, each player is only allowed to play one hand at a time. This variant allows players to play multiple hands during the round instead of just one.

Unlike the usual variant where six to eight decks of cards are used in the game, this variant is played with only a single deck of cards.
