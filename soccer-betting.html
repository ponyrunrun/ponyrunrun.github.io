Football Betting Markets

As more bookmakers become creative, it is only natural for betting markets to expand. Here are some of the more popular bet types for football betting.

1
Match Winner
This wager is placed on the team the player thinks will be the eventual winner of the match. In this wager, they can choose to bet on home, draw, or away.
2
Tournament Winner
This wager is placed on a specific team to win the tournament, be it in the form of a domestic, international, or league cup.
3
Correct Score
This wager involves predicting the final score. Since the chances of winning this bet is very low, players who bet on this will have a high payout.
4
Over or Under Goals
This wager is placed based on the number of goals that will happen in the match. The bet will be on a number with a decimal point. For example, a bet of over 3.5 means that the player will need to see 4 or more goals to win the bet.
5
Anytime Goalscorer
This wager involves betting on any team member to score at any time during the match. Punters can bet on the first or last goalscorer of the match.
6
Both Teams to Score
A type of wager involving bets placed to see if both teams will score in the match. The type of bets includes betting on both teams to score, or betting on both teams not to score.
7
Handicap Match Result
This wager involves betting on a team to win or lose by a specific margin. For example, you bet on your team to lose by two goals or more.
8
Second Half Result
This involves placing bets on which team will win the second half of the match. Regardless of the first match or final score, this bet only considers the results of the second match.
9
Draw No Bet
In this bet, if you place a bet on one team to win the game and the match is a draw, your bet is void. The payout for this type of bet is lower than that of a match winner bet.
10
Double Chance
This wager involves betting on two outcomes of the match. You can bet on one team to win or draw, or for both teams to win.
11
First Team to Score
This wager involves placing bets on which team will be the first to score in the match.
12
Number of Corners
This wager involves betting on how many corners will happen during the match.

Types of
Football Bets

In sports betting, bookmakers set the odds by calculating how much to pay clients in the event a bet is won. It is a sophisticated process that uses the combination of human knowledge, instinct, and scientific data.

Football betting odds represent the likelihood of an outcome. That way, when you place a bet, you need to calculate if the probability is likely to take place. You should try to place bets with odds that you believe that have good value.

If you are a seasoned football bettor, you can place bets through multiple sites as each online betting site has their own specialty. Here are some of the different types of football bets available:

Fixed Odds Betting

The commonest type of bets with any bookmaker, the concept is that the odds remain the same despite the changes or activity of the market at a later time.

Lay Betting

This type of bet can be placed on a betting exchange site. When a lay bet is placed, you are betting against the outcome to occur. The amount you bet would be the amount you would win. For example, if you were to bet $5 and win, you would win $5 and a return of your bet, giving you a total of $10.

Accumulator Betting

A parlay or accumulator bet are often used by recreational players in the hopes of getting lucky. This type of wager allows you to select multiple selectors and can has the potential to give a gigantic return.

In Play Betting

As the name suggests, this wager involves placing a bet once the game is in play. This can give bettors a better idea as they have the opportunity to see how the game has started before placing their bet. Multiple betting systems are now available as the markets fluctuate significantly once the game is in play. Those with some skill and knowledge of how the market works can take advantage of this betting style.

Handicap Betting

This type of wager depends on the outcome of the match but with a preset margin. These types of bets make a lot of sense when there is an obvious favorite in the match so bettors can get better odds and returns on their wagers.

Soccer Betting Tips

There are many things to consider when placing a bet on a soccer match such as taking into consideration the statistics and league table. While it can be easy to look at basic stats and think one team is likely to win, there are many occasions where an underdog wins the match. Sometimes, it is down to specific game plans, the competitiveness of the league, and more. Here are some of the football betting tips to help start you off:

League Specific Tips

Each league has specific characteristics and understanding each nuance can be to your benefit.
Here are some facts that can influence the bets you place:

1
The Super League
A European elite football competition that starts with two groups of ten teams, going on to four teams in four groups, all the way to the knockout phase. The finale is played on neutral ground annually.
2
League One
A league that has been recently dominated by Paris Saint Germain, potentially due to their financial muscle. Although not as competitive as the other leagues, they tend to play on the offensive and consist of some of the world’s biggest talents.
3
Europa League
This competition is for teams that fall short of qualifying for the Champions league. Bigger clubs who end up in this competition tend to field weaker teams and do not prioritize the tournament, offering great odds for underdogs.
4
Champions League
One of the most prestigious tournaments throughout Europe, the winners in this league are often no surprise. Real Madrid has won the most times followed by AC Milan and Liverpool.
5
Serie A
Having a great reputation for defense, their styles are changing in recent years. Now, the number of teams competing at the top shows that this is one of the most competitive leagues in the world.
6
Bundesliga
A team that often promotes young footballers and managers, eyes are frequently on Bayern Munich as they are believed to have all the money and buys players from main rivals.
7
Premier League
The English Premier League (EPL) is one of the most competitive football campaigns as it features some of the biggest clubs including Liverpool, Chelsea, Manchester City, Manchester United, and more. While some of these clubs are the most famous, there are sometimes upsets which add a little twist and fun for many bettors. Drawing fans from all over the world, there are 380 matches every season! In this league, it is not uncommon to see underdogs beat out the others to emerge in the top 6.
8
La Liga
This league is mostly dominated by two teams: Real Madrid and Barcelona. If at home, it is rare for these two teams to lose.
Team Specific Tips
When betting on over or under goals, consider the team’s play style. An attacking team playing against another attacking team tends to lead to a high number of goals. However, an attacking team playing against an opponent
intent on shutting them out will result in fewer goals. In these situations, choosing to bet on “both teams to score” could lead you to winning some
money.
Player Specific Tips
When placing bets on individuals, consider how they perform against other teams. Statistics do not lie. If you see a player who scores a lot but very seldomly in big matches, bet wisely. This can also work the other way round as the teams place players based on their ability to compete against the players on the other team.

Online Gambling Law
in Singapore

In Singapore, the Remote Gambling Act (RGA) refers to betting, gaming, and lottery in general. Under this act, the betting experience refers to the staking of money on the outcome of a sports event while the Common Gaming Houses Act (CGHA) refers to games that involve a mix of chance and skill.

In Singapore, only Singapore Pool and Singapore Turf Club hold licenses to offer gambling or betting to Singaporeans. Since both these establishments have limited options, many punters move ahead with bookies and online bookmakers for a more fulfilling experience.

While the RGA discourages online gambling, there are some legal experts who agree that it is not illegal to play online games from one’s own home. However, as an added insurance policy, it is best to use virtual private networks (VPNs).
