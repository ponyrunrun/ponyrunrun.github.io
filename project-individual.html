What is Bored Ape Yacht Club (BAYC)?

The Bored Ape Yacht Club (BAYC) is a collection of 10,000 collectable NFTs generated programmatically from 170 distinct qualities. An NFT is simply a way for digital goods ownership to be represented on the blockchain. BAYC’s artwork includes cartoonish apes with unique features and odd qualities.

While each ape is unique, some have qualities that increase the ape’s value. The most desired features are laser eyes, gold fur, and a king’s crown. They are ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum network. Each Bored Ape artwork also functions as a Yacht Club membership card, granting access to members-only services. The BAYC is a project of Yuga Labs, based in the United States.

Bored Ape Yacht Club (BAYC) Review: History

BAYC was launched in April 2021 by four pseudonymous developers, Gargamel, Gordon Goner, Emperor Tomato Ketchup, and No Sass. Users were given NFT coupons but did not have the opportunity to view the art at first. As a result, the project received little interest and did not sell out. However, the initiative soon sold out as the art was sent, and people saw their NFTs for the first time. Jimmy Fallon, DJ Khaled, and Stephen Curry are among the celebrities that possess a BAYC NFT and use it as their social media profile photo.


What are the Benefits of Owning a Bored Ape Yacht Club NFT?

  • When you hold a Bored Ape NFT, you not only own the digital asset, but your token also functions as a “ticket” to the Yacht Club.
  • Few investors put money into BAYC with the expectation of earning a profit. As BAYC becomes more popular, their prices will increase. As a result, investors will buy apes with the idea of later selling them for a profit.
  • An ape as a social profile image symbolizes affluence and crypto awareness.
  • Holders also have access to future collections, airdrops, and other benefits. For example, all Bored Ape Yacht Club members received mutant serum through an airdrop. The Mutant Ape Yacht Club is a method of awarding Bored Ape holders with a completely new NFT.

How to Sell Bored Ape Yacht Club NFT?

The simplest way to sell a Bored Ape NFT is at OpenSea. Here is a step-by-step guide to sell BAYC NFT at OpenSea:

  • Visit the OpenSea website and login into your account.
  • In the top right-hand corner, click Profile.
  • Browse your NFT collection and select the NFT you want to sell. Select the ‘Sell‘ option at the top right-hand corner of the page to be taken to the listing page.
  • Select how you want to sell your NFT. Set the price, auction type, sale duration, and buyer type.
  • Choose how long your item will be available for sale. The default settings are one day, three days, or one week, but you may use the calendar to select whatever period you like.
  • Pay fees and complete your NFT listing.

A popup will appear after completing the steps, requiring you to confirm a signature request to finalize your NFT listing. That’s it! You have successfully put your NFT on OpenSea for sale! It will display in your Profile under the ‘Activity’ page.

Is it a smart idea to invest in the Bored Ape Yacht Club?

Why would someone invest in NFT? In the same way as evidence of ownership is crucial in the offline world, it is vital in the online world. NFT is essentially a token that proves you hold the proof of ownership. You have a potentially valuable digital asset if you have evidence of ownership because you are the only one who genuinely possesses the original. However, there are additional reasons why BAYC NFTs can be valuable, such as:

  • Only 10,000 Bored Apes were created. As a result, there is limited availability.
  • The last and most essential aspect is the community that has grown up around a BAYC collection. BAYC Alternatives

Cryptopunk:

CryptoPunks, released in 2017, is considered one of the first NFT collections. The project comprises 10,000 randomly produced 24×24 pixel images with random characteristics such as a person wearing a hat or smoking a pipe.

Prime Ape Planet PAP:

Prime Ape Planet has attracted a whopping 100,000+ Discord members in less than 24 hours. It is unusual in the NFT space. The project’s high worth stems from a mix of high-quality art and metaverse-ready design.

Doodles:

CLONE X – X TAKASHI MURAKAMI:

Conclusion

Some of you may believe NFTs are absurd but the Bored Apes aren’t going away anytime soon. In the realm of NFTs, the Bored Ape Yacht Club is an exciting development.
