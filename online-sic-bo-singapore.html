Sic Bo Betting Guide

Though similar to other Asian dice games, such as Big and Small, Sic Bo is a far more complex game that requires a significant amount of skill. Unlike most traditional gambling games where all is a game of chance, in Sic Bo, it’s all about predicting future probabilities using an optimal betting strategy.

The online casino game starts with players placing bets on the possible dice combination. After bets are closed, the dealer shakes the dice and the total score is the final result. Here are some of the types of bets available.

Any Number

One of the simplest sic bo bets, the player predicts what will appear on the faces of the 3 dice. If it happens, the player wins. The payout is based on the number of dice that appear in the prediction.

For example, if the player bets on a single number such as 5. If the rolled dice shows a number 5, the player wins a 1 : 1 ratio. If there are two dice that have the number 5, the player wins twice their bet. Likewise, if all three dice has the number 5, the payout is three times the player’s bet.

3 Dice Total

As the name of the bet suggests, players bet on the total score of the three dice. This bet comprises of 14 different betting options. The payout for this bet ranges from 6 : 1 to 50 : 1. This is the most common type of sic bo bet at most tables.

In this bet, there are some bets that have extremely high payouts. For example, the sum of 4 or 17 has the highest payout with a 15.28% house edge while a total of 10 or 11 has the lowest payouts at 6 : 1.

Small and Big

Another common bet among many sic bo players, this bet involves the player wagering on the total score of the 3 dice. In this bet, players can choose to bet on “Over” for a score of 11 to 17 or “Under” for 4 to 10 points.

In this bet, the payout is much lower at a ratio of 1 : 1 as the odds of winning and losing are 50 : 50. Although it has a lower payout, this bet is favored due to their low house edge of 2.78%. Totals of 3 and 18 are excluded from this bet.

Double and Triple

In double bets, you predict that two of a number will show up, simply put, a pair. For example, if you choose 3 to be the winning number, you win when the outcome of the dice shows a 3, 3, x. The payout for this bet is 10 : 1.

Similar to the double bet, players bet on a specific number to appear three times in triple bets. For example, if you chose 5 to be the winning number, you win if the dice shows 5, 5, 5. The payout for this rate is much higher but differs between online casinos.

Sic Bo Betting Strategy
There are many different betting strategies available. The most important sic bo strategy to
remember is to select a bet type that maximizes your winnings. Here are some tips and tricks:
Low Risk Strategy
Beginners should stick to a strategy that has the lowest house edge. Although these bets do not have high payout rates, it is safe and gives you more time on the sic bo table. In this case, stay with small and big bets.
Medium Risk Strategy
Best for intermediate and seasoned players, this technique uses a combination of numbers so that you select at least four winning combinations. This combina–tion bets so each spin offers you four chances of winning.
