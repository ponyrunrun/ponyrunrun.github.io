Basic NBA Betting Strategy
The basics are vital for both beginners and seasoned bettors.
Although it may seem trivial or simple, these fundamentals could make or break your bank.
Don’t Overcomplicate Betting
Keep your bets simple. There seems to be a general consensus that simple bets have worse odds and will not be profitable. However, the odds are set by betting experts and your best odds at winning are through simple bets.
Don’t Be Swayed By The Betting Public
Its easy to get swayed by the crowd especially if it’s a hyped-up match. As thousands of analysts announce their predictions and guarantees, bear in mind that they would be rich betting on their predictions if they are always right! Take all information with a grain of salt.
Bet What You Know
Bet What You Know Stick to what you know. There is wisdom in this old saying. If you are an expert on a specific team, place your bets on the team you know instead of risking it on a team you know little about. If you are great at predicting winners, stick to the NBA Moneylines bet!
Always Double Check Your Betting
To err is human and there is no doubt that we are only human. Regardless of how careful you usually are, always double check your bets because it can become a very costly mistake. You might just have forfeited your winnings by accidentally placing a wrong bet.

Popular NBA Betting Markets
Getting to know the popular NBA betting markets helps you to build a strategy around a solid option. In the long run, sticking to one betting market is often the best choice. However, you should stay alert about bets with good potential. Here are some of the most popular NBA betting markets:
NBA Moneylines
This moneyline market is the best choice if you simply want to pick a winning team. In this bet, you simply have to pick the franchise you think will win. Since most sites look at the end result that includes the extra time, there is no tie.
NBA Point Spreads
NBA spreads are best for punters who prefer a more balanced option. This is great for situations when there is a huge disparity between the two opposing teams. In this bet, the weaker team gets an advantage to start the match on even terms.
NBA Totals
This is a bet where the winning team does not matter. Instead, bets are placed on the number of points scored in each game. One of the most popular NBA markets, all you need to do is predict the total points scored by both teams at the end of the match.
NBA Props, Futures, and Outrights
One of the more special bets, this option is more complex as betting sites allow wagers to be placed on different markets such as for a specific player to score a number of points, a team to win the NBA and more.
